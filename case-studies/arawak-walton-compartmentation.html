Project Overview

Arawak Walton engaged Holistic Fire Safety to conduct an initial compartmentation survey at an independent living scheme for over-55s in Manchester. This was prompted by concerns raised by a previous contractor. Holistic’s thorough and professional approach provided clarity and reassurance through a validation site meeting and multiple consultation calls.

Key Deliverables

Initial & Expanded Surveys

  • Conducted an initial compartmentation survey at one independent living scheme.
  • Due to high-quality assessment, commissioned to carry out six additional surveys across Arawak Walton’s independent living schemes.

Survey Objectives

  • Assessed fire separation between flats, common areas, and ancillary spaces.
  • Identified potential routes for heat and smoke transfer.

Survey Documentation & Compliance

  • Utilized Bolster Systems, a digital platform, to record findings and pinpoint defect locations.
  • Generated comprehensive PDF reports, providing clear scope details for senior management and contractors.

Detailed Reporting & Remediation Recommendations

  • Mapped each penetration onto building layout plans.
  • Documented the surface, substrate, and size of each opening.
  • Recommended appropriate remediation materials aligned with building regulations and manufacturer-tested solutions (Protecta and others).

Outcome & Impact

  • Provided clear, actionable insights into fire safety compliance.
  • Enabled senior management to make informed decisions on fire safety improvements.
  • Ensured contractors had precise, well-documented scopes of work.
  • Strengthened fire safety strategies across independent living schemes.
  • Increased confidence in regulatory compliance and overall resident safety.

Holistic Fire Safety’s expertise, attention to detail, and methodical approach have been instrumental in enhancing fire safety across Arawak Walton’s properties, ensuring compliance and resident protection.
