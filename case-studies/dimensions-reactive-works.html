Project Overview

Dimensions is a nationwide organization supporting individuals with learning disabilities and autism, ensuring they live safely and happily. Holistic Fire Safety was engaged in 2022 to provide fire door replacements, remedial works, and a nationwide reactive service.

Key Deliverables

Initial Scope & Compliance Alignment

  • Held an initial project meeting in August 2022 to define client objectives and priorities.
  • Aligned works with statutory regulations: RRFSO 2005, BSA 2022, FSA 2021, and FSER 2022.

Risk-Based & Proportionate Approach

  • Developed a strategic remediation plan to address fire door non-compliance.
  • Prioritized remediation over replacement where feasible, ensuring cost-effective solutions.

Expansion to Nationwide Service

  • Evolved from a localized project into a nationwide reactive service.
  • Successfully completed 300+ reactive call-outs, with most jobs addressed within 7 days of reporting.

Reactive Works Approach

First Time Fix Priority

  • Aimed to complete repairs on the first visit wherever possible.
  • If repairs could not be completed, conducted a detailed survey documenting the issue.
  • Provided photographic evidence and a proposed solution for further action.

Planned Works & Nationwide Execution

Over 150 properties received planned fire safety improvements, including:

  • Fire Door Remediations to enhance existing fire safety infrastructure.
  • Fire Door Replacements where remediation was unviable.
  • Fire Stopping to ensure effective compartmentation.

Outcome & Impact

  • Enhanced fire safety compliance across Dimensions properties nationwide.
  • Efficient turnaround, minimizing risks by addressing issues promptly.
  • Cost-effective approach, ensuring remediation was prioritized where viable.
  • Long-term partnership, expanding services based on demonstrated reliability and expertise.

Holistic Fire Safety continues to support Dimensions with responsive and planned fire safety solutions, ensuring their properties remain compliant and safe for residents.
