Project Overview

Hull City Council engaged Holistic Fire Safety to carry out a comprehensive fire door inspection programme in line with the Fire Safety (England) Regulations 2022. Holistic’s professionalism, technical expertise, and deep understanding of regulatory compliance enabled the council to effectively meet fire safety requirements while addressing the unique challenges faced by local authorities.

Key Deliverables

Fire Door Inspections & Compliance

  • Conducted thorough assessments of fire doors within high-risk residential buildings.
  • Provided detailed reports to support remedial actions and compliance improvements.

Solution-Driven Approach

  • Leveraged extensive local knowledge to efficiently address compliance needs.
  • Utilized a structured methodology to ensure accuracy and consistency.

Regulatory Compliance Support

  • Ensured alignment with Fire Safety (England) Regulations 2022.
  • Provided clear, actionable recommendations for remedial works.

Execution & Communication

  • Maintained excellent communication with Hull City Council throughout the programme.
  • Held regular progress meetings to ensure transparency and project efficiency.
  • Delivered the project on time and within budget, reinforcing the council’s fire safety strategy.

Outcome & Impact

  • Strengthened fire safety compliance across high-risk residential buildings.
  • Provided well-documented assessments to facilitate remedial actions.
  • Enabled the council to effectively prioritize resident safety.
  • Established a reliable and professional partnership, ensuring long-term fire safety improvements.

Holistic Fire Safety’s structured and methodical approach provided Hull City Council with confidence in regulatory compliance, fire door safety, and risk mitigation, ensuring enhanced safety for residents.
