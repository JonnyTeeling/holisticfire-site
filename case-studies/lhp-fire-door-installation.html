Lincolnshire Housing Partnership

Extensive fire door installations and remedial works across Lincolnshire Housing Partnership properties.

390Certified GRP flat entrance doors installed

470+Fire door remediations using BM Trada Q Mark ART principles

Project Overview

In September 2022, Holistic Fire Safety was awarded a contract through the En-Procure Framework to carry out extensive fire door installations and remedial works across Lincolnshire Housing Partnership properties.

Key Deliverables

Property Types Covered

  • General Needs Premises – Communal fire doors & ancillary accommodation fire doors, including service risers and cupboard doors.
  • Sheltered & Assisted Living Schemes – Communal fire doors and ancillary fire doors within operational kitchens, laundries, plant rooms, and service risers.

Fire Door Installations & Remediations

  • Installed 390 x Certified GRP Flat Entrance Doors.
  • Replaced 287 x Certified Communal & Ancillary Timber Fire Doors.
  • Conducted 470+ Fire Door Remediations using BM Trada Q Mark ART principles.
  • Performed extensive fire stopping across the residential stock.

Outcome & Impact

  • Delivered high-quality fire safety solutions, ensuring compliance across multiple LHP properties.
  • Improved fire resilience and safety in general needs and assisted living accommodations.
  • Provided efficient and compliant remedial works, ensuring fire doors met the highest safety standards.
  • Achieved client satisfaction, leading to LHP exercising a 2-year contract extension after successfully delivering consistent service.

Holistic Fire Safety remains a trusted partner for Lincolnshire Housing Partnership, ensuring ongoing fire safety compliance and resident protection.
