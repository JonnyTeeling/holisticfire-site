Project Overview

Holistic Fire Safety has been Silva Homes’ trusted Fire Safety Consultant since 2021, delivering comprehensive fire risk assessments and passive fire protection solutions.

Key Deliverables

Fire Risk Assessments (2021-2024)

  • Completed all T4 Fire Risk Assessments followed by T3 Assessments.

Fire Door Inspections (2023)

  • Conducted 4,224 Fire Door Inspections across Silva Homes’ housing stock.
  • Covered all 300 low-rise general needs blocks and 13 higher-risk buildings.

Fire Door Remedial Works (2024)

  • Collaborated with Silva Homes’ technical and compliance teams to establish remedial strategies in the absence of planned refurbishments until 2026.
  • Applied risk ratings to prioritize doors requiring urgent attention.
  • Implemented BM Trada ART principles to bring doors as close to compliance as possible.
  • Carried out fire door repairs and maintenance on approximately 750 fire doorsets.
  • Ensured compliance with RRFSO Article 18 (Maintenance) & Article 4 (General Fire Precautions).

Outcome & Impact

  • Enhanced fire safety compliance across Silva Homes’ housing stock.
  • Improved fire door performance, reducing risk in higher-priority areas.
  • Supported long-term fire safety planning in alignment with future refurbishment schedules.
  • Provided a structured approach to fire door maintenance, ensuring regulatory compliance and resident safety.

Holistic Fire Safety continues to work closely with Silva Homes, delivering proactive fire safety solutions and ensuring robust compliance across their estate.
